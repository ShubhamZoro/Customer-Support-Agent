=============================================================
  SHOPWAVE E-COMMERCE — OFFICIAL REFUND & RETURN POLICY
  Version 5.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025
=============================================================

1. RETURN WINDOWS BY PRODUCT CATEGORY
---------------------------------------
Return eligibility is determined by the product category of the order,
calculated from the order_date stored in the system (NOT the delivery date).

1.1 Electronics           : 30 days from order date.
1.2 Clothing & Apparel    : 15 days from order date.
1.3 Home & Living         : 30 days from order date.
1.4 Books                 : 14 days from order date.
1.5 Toys & Games          : 21 days from order date.
1.6 Subscriptions         :  7 days from order date (non-refundable after first use).
1.7 Services              :  7 days from order date (non-refundable once service begins).

NOTE: There are no premium tiers or plan-based extensions. Every customer
is subject to the same category-based window regardless of account status.

2. CONDITIONS ALWAYS ELIGIBLE FOR RETURN (NO WINDOW LIMIT)
-----------------------------------------------------------
The following reasons bypass the return window entirely:

2.1 Item is defective or malfunctioning — always eligible.
2.2 Item was damaged during shipping — eligible if reported within 72 hours of delivery.
2.3 Wrong item received (not what was ordered) — always eligible.
2.4 Item not received despite delivery confirmation — eligible after investigation.

3. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS (ABSOLUTE RESTRICTIONS)
-------------------------------------------------
The following items cannot be refunded under any circumstances:

3.1 Gift Card payments — orders paid entirely with a Gift Card are non-refundable.
    (Gift Card purchases may receive store credit at agent discretion.)
3.2 Subscription or Service items after first use — non-refundable once activated.
3.3 Personalized or custom-made items — non-refundable unless defective.
3.4 Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants) — non-refundable.
3.5 Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at purchase — non-refundable.
3.6 Digital goods (e-books, software licenses) after download/activation.

4. DUPLICATE REFUND RESTRICTION
---------------------------------
4.1 Each order may only be refunded ONCE.
4.2 If an order already has a return_status of "Refund Initiated" or "Returned",
    no further refund requests will be accepted for that order.
4.3 Customers attempting to refund an already-refunded order will be informed
    of the existing refund record.

5. USER OWNERSHIP & SECURITY
------------------------------
5.1 Customers must be logged in to initiate a refund.
5.2 A refund can only be initiated for orders that belong to the logged-in user's account.
5.3 Customers cannot initiate refunds on behalf of other users.
5.4 The agent will verify order ownership before processing any refund action.

6. REFUND PROCESS
------------------
6.1 Customer provides their Order ID and reason for the return.
6.2 The agent verifies ownership (order belongs to the logged-in user).
6.3 The agent checks return window eligibility based on product category.
6.4 The agent checks for duplicate refund attempts.
6.5 The agent checks payment method restrictions (e.g. Gift Card).
6.6 If approved: order status is updated to "Refund Initiated" and a
    confirmation email is sent to the customer's registered email address.
6.7 If denied: the customer is shown the specific policy reason on screen.

7. PROCESSING TIMELINE BY PAYMENT METHOD
------------------------------------------
7.1 Credit Card : 5–10 business days to appear on statement.
7.2 Debit Card  : 3–5 business days.
7.3 PayPal      : 1–3 business days.
7.4 Gift Card   : Non-refundable (see Section 3.1).

8. RETURN SHIPPING
-------------------
8.1 Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless:
    (a) The item was defective or damaged during shipping, OR
    (b) The wrong item was sent.
8.2 ShopWave will provide a prepaid return label for company-error returns.

9. PARTIAL REFUNDS
-------------------
9.1 Items returned in used or opened condition may receive a partial refund
    (50–75% of the product price) at agent discretion.
9.2 Missing accessories or damaged original packaging: up to 25% deduction.
9.3 Any discount applied to the original order will be deducted from the refund amount.

10. FRAUD PREVENTION
---------------------
10.1 Customers with more than 3 refund requests in any 90-day rolling window
     are flagged for manual review.
10.2 Suspicious patterns (e.g. "item not received" on confirmed-delivered orders)
     will be escalated to the customer trust team.

11. AGENT AUTHORITY
--------------------
11.1 This AI agent can approve or deny refunds within the rules above.
11.2 Refunds cannot be processed if the order does not belong to the logged-in user.
11.3 Policy exceptions require human supervisor approval and cannot be granted
     by this agent.
=============================================================
